The Origin

Tabchilli is crafted using carefully selected fine ingredients, handpicked & handmade using traditions passed on through generations. From picking the raw ingredients to making Tabchilli, the process goes through a long wild fermentation process that allows the earthy, spicy & tangy flavours to merge together to form a rich, complex and delightfully floral woody heat. We then move to in the infusion of extra virgin olive oil, adding a depth of texture allowing the heat to steadily build and linger with each taste. Tabchilli does not overwhelm the existing flavour or texture of your food; it pairs perfectly by adding another journey to your culinary enjoyment.

Step 1

Thai Chilli Bird

Also known as Thaichillibird birdchiles or bird's-eye chiles, or prikkeenoo, these tiny 1 to 1 1/2-inch #chilli’s range in color from green to red. On the Scoville scale, #BirdsEyeChilis are at 100,000 Scoville Heat Units. This is to be classified with a degree of sharpness of 9. For comparison: Tabasco Chili pods are only half hot with 50,000 Scoville. Thai Birdseye chili peppers can shoot the bird with up to 225,000 SHU. But for #tabchilli we use only Ripe pods which are about 100,000 Scoville hot.
